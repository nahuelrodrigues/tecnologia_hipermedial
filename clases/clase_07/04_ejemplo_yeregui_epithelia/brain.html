Mundus Imaginabilis

...in one case as in another, defined terms escape his definition, Dorita A is not Dorita A, or Lucas B is not Lucas B. And starting from an instantaneous relationship A=B, or B=A, the fission of the crust of the real is given in a chain. Perhaps when the papillae of A rub delectably against the mucosae of B, everything is rubbing against something else and playing another game and scorching dictionaries. The time for a moan, of course, but Hyde and Jekyll look at each other face to face in a relationship of A»»B / B»»A.

(Cortázar)
